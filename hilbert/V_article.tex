\section*{Noether bridge (index-free API)}
We introduce a lightweight primitive:
\[
  \NoetherTensor{L}{\U}{\Xi},
\]
mapping a local density \(L\), field \(\U\), and infinitesimal generator \(\Xi\) to an
abstract current \(\Current\) (and, for translations, a stress--energy \(\StressEnergy\)),
with conservation
\[
  \Div{\Current} = 0.
\]
The closure \GTwoStatement{} supplies the stationarity hypothesis required to invoke this construction.

\section*{Volume 4: invariants and statistical closure}
Admissible coarse-grainings (consistent with \(\C\)) cannot increase distinguishability:
\[
  \KL{P}{Q}\ \text{does not increase under admissible maps.}
\]
At the true parameter, the score has zero mean:
\[
  \GFourScoreZero.
\]
Estimation accuracy is bounded via \(\Fisher\), while symmetry induces index-free invariants
\(\Invariant{\Xi}\) consistent with \(\Div{\Current}=0\). Residual structure \(\Res\) is thereby
interpreted as constrained noise under the established closures.
