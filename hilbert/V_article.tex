\begin{abstract}
This note declares the shared symbols and three contracts used across the build, and
introduces an index-free Noether bridge (\NoetherTensor{L}{\U}{\Xi}) to connect the
closure \GTwoStatement{} to conserved currents. All statement handles are title-safe
(\verb|\ensuremath|), so they can be used directly in text, headings, and captions.
\end{abstract}

\section*{Noether bridge (index-free API)}
We introduce a lightweight primitive:
\[
  \NoetherTensor{L}{\U}{\Xi},
\]
mapping a local density \(L\), field \(\U\), and infinitesimal generator \(\Xi\) to an
abstract current \(\Current\) (and, for translations, a stress–energy \(\StressEnergy\)),
with conservation
\[
  \Div{\Current} = 0.
\]
The closure \GTwoStatement{} supplies the stationarity hypothesis needed to invoke this construction.
